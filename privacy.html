Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

AI Guesser is designed with privacy first. We don't collect, sell, or share your personal data.

What we collect

AI Guesser stores your scores, streaks, player name, and game history entirely on your device. No personal information is collected or sent to any server.

No accounts required

You can play without creating an account. No email address or phone number is ever requested. The optional player name you enter is stored locally on your device only.

Local storage only

All data (high scores, daily streaks, wrong-answer history, player name) is saved locally using AsyncStorage on iOS/Android and localStorage on web. Uninstalling the app removes all data.
